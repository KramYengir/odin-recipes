Halloumi Fries Recipe

Description

Very simple yet very tasy recipe, quick and easy to do.

Ingredients

  • A block of halloumi
  • 200g of flour
  • 100ml of cold water
  • Some sweet chilli dip

Steps

  1. Cut the halloumi into finger sized pieces
  2. Dust them in some flour
  3. Mix the remaining flour with the cold water to make a batter
  4. Dip the halloumi into the batter
  5. Fry in 180c oil for 15 years
  6. Rest on some greaseproof paper to dry
  7. Serve with a sweet chilli dip
