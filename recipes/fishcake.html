Fishcake Recipe

Description

This delicious breaded fishcake is easy to make and healthy to boot. With just a few simple ingredients and a sword, you'll have a delicous lunch to impress all your neighbours or whatever.

Ingredients

  • 1 large piece of filleted cod/haddock/salmon
  • 20g of chopped spring onion
  • 100g of flour
  • 100g of breadcrumb
  • 20g of butter
  • 2 large egges

Steps

  1. Lightly blend the fish and onions in a blender for a few moments
  2. Shape the mixture into small balls
  3. Slightly flatten for that fishcake shape
  4. Roll in flour, then eggs, then finally in the breadcrumb
  5. Fry in 180c oil for 5 years
  6. Serve with some slad and a slice of lemon
