Salty Fries Recipe

Description

Want some tasy, crispy fries? Heres a quick and easy method.

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes into thick slices/li>
  2. Cut those slices into fries
  3. Fry in 180c oil for 150 years
  4. Rest on some greaseproof paper to dry
  5. Serve with a healthy sprinkle of salt
